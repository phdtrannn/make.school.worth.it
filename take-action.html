Take Action

Programs such as the Youth Skills and Innovation Initiative launched by the Intel Corporation and the Global Business Coalition for Education aim to prepare students with basic professional skills.

Students can join the Youth Council and become a Global Youth Ambassador. The group advises on skills development and implements recommendations to build an effective model that addresses the problems faced in education.
